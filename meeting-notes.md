### Ideas Discussed
- **Idea 4 (Freelancer support tool):** Discussed but concern raised that ChatGPT already covers most of it, differentiation unclear.
- **Idea 6 (Farmer advisor AI):** Large market, but reaching farmers within the Week 1 deadline seen as too risky, despite Sajid's agriculture university contacts.

- Integration potential (e.g. Shopify or order sheet syncing) differentiates it from basic chatbot auto-replies

- Muhammad suggested the product could evolve into a unified inbox pulling DMs from Instagram, WhatsApp, and Facebook.
- Sajid raised a trust concern: why would sellers connect Shopify/order data to an unknown team's product? Flagged for user interviews.
- Agreed: narrow and sharp beats broad and vague.
- Agreed: avoid surveys (easily gamed); prioritize real proof, shop visit photos, actual DM conversations, interview notes.

- Final decision to be made June 26 after a follow-up call

## Meeting 2 - June 26, 2026

**Attendees:** Muhammad Ullah Baig, Khansa Maryam, Sajid Islam, Manahil Shah
**Duration:** ~51 minutes
**Video Recording:** [Week 1 Meeting - Idea Selection](https://fathom.video/share/z7zyvbdTLvDoTqdxtW1BxGhv_-fcFgMd)

### Meeting Purpose
Align on a final project idea and define next steps for user research before the Sunday submission deadline.

### Idea Finalised
**Idea 8:** AI-powered social commerce support agent for Instagram and WhatsApp sellers, niche focus on fashion and clothing brands. Chosen for feasibility within 8 weeks and immediate user accessibility.

### Key Discussion Points
- Muhammad presented research on Ideas 1, 5, and 8; Idea 8 most practical to build and deliver within 8 weeks.
- Sajid outlined the core workflow: chatbot handling pre-order questions, COD confirmations, delivery updates, returns, and cancellations. Urdu-based gives it a local edge over international tools.
- Khansa shared live outreach findings from messaging 16 Pakistani clothing brands on Instagram. Large brands redirected to websites, smaller brands were more accessible, only one brand answered all three questions manually, validating the pain point.
- Debated narrow niche vs. staying broad. Decided to focus on fashion/clothing initially, with an architecture designed to be customizable for other industries later.
- Agreed cold pitching via Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, and email is the primary outreach method; smaller brands respond better.
- Interview focus areas identified: delivery issues, returns, cancellations, order status inquiries, and how sellers currently handle DMs manually.

### Decisions Made
- Idea 8 confirmed as final choice
- Niche narrowed to fashion and clothing brands
- Minimum 5 user interviews to be conducted collectively before Sunday submission
- GitHub repository to be used for all research and documentation from Week 2
- Rotating team lead structure confirmed, first lead assigned Sunday

### Action Items
- Sajid: generate interview questions from research, share with squad
- All members: begin outreach to fashion brands immediately, share interview notes in group
- All members: post daily progress updates in team group
- Squad: Sunday check-in to assign first team lead, review outreach progress, finalize submission form
