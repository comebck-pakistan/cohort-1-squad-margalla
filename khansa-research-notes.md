## Answering the Agenda Questions

### Which idea do you support?
Idea 8, backed by live evidence collected the same day, not just desk research.

### Why is it the best option?
I messaged 16 Pakistani clothing brands on Instagram before this meeting. Key findings:

- Large accounts like garoor_pk (162k), El_bellara (136k), and apparelspickup (133k) either didn't reply or replied late with no real answers.
- Brands that replied instantly used auto-replies answering zero of the three questions, just greetings, website redirects, or WhatsApp community links.
- Only Fatima Clothing (44.2k followers) answered all three questions manually within the same minute, meaning a real person was sitting there waiting, which doesn't scale with a large following.
- Glamifyclosetofficial (78.9k) sent an auto-greeting, then a human replied manually 30 minutes later with full answers. This is the most telling data point: auto-reply buys time, then a human catches up. That human cost is exactly what this tool eliminates.
- Erums Collection redirected me to a WhatsApp community instead of answering directly.

This is a live, current problem, not theoretical. Brands handling DMs well are doing it through manual human effort, which doesn't scale.

### How will the tool work?
An AI agent sits inside the seller's Instagram and WhatsApp DMs. When a customer asks about size, delivery city, or timeframe, the agent reads the message in Urdu, Roman Urdu, or English, checks the seller's actual catalog, and replies instantly with a real answer. When a customer wants to order, the agent handles COD confirmation and logs the order into the seller's system. Complex cases escalate to the owner.

Unlike glamifyclosetofficial's current setup (human needed after 30 minutes), this tool responds in seconds without human involvement.

### Who are the target customers?
Small to mid-size Pakistani clothing brands (1k–150k followers) taking orders manually through Instagram and WhatsApp DMs. Today's data reflects exactly this profile.

### How can we reach those customers?
16 brands were messaged in under an hour today just by searching Pakistani clothing brands on Instagram. No other shortlisted idea offers this level of immediate access to real users. Five user interviews are achievable before the weekend.

### Problems flagged during outreach

- Not always clear if the account is run by the owner or an employee.
- Cold messaging converts poorly compared to cold calling.
- Shomiofficialpk likely deflected with a corporate non-answer.

- Better to prioritize brands with owner information in bio, so outreach reaches the relevant decision-maker instead of customer service.
