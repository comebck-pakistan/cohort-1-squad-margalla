# Customer Discovery Interview Questions

## Context

These questions are for validating Squad Margalla's product idea: **The Social-Commerce Support Agent**.

The target users are Pakistani online sellers, especially clothing brand owners, who receive customer messages and orders through Instagram, WhatsApp, Shopify, WooCommerce, Daraz, or similar channels.

The goal is to understand their real workflow, repeated customer questions, COD confirmation process, lost orders, failed deliveries, and repetitive tasks.

---

## Interview Questions

1. What do you sell, and where do most of your customers come from?

2. Do you mainly sell through Instagram, WhatsApp, Shopify, WooCommerce, Daraz, or something else?

3. Roughly how many orders do you receive in a normal month?

4. Walk me through what happens after a customer messages you or places an order.

5. What customer question do you answer again and again every day?

6. What part of handling customer messages or orders wastes the most time?

7. What usually causes lost orders, cancelled orders, failed deliveries, or returns?

8. How do you currently confirm Cash on Delivery (COD) orders and verify customer addresses?

9. How do you currently handle "Where is my order?" messages after shipping?

10. If you could remove only one repetitive task from your business today, what would it be and why?

---

## Why These Questions Matter

These questions help us discover:

- Which sales channels sellers actually use
- How many orders they handle
- Which customer questions repeat the most
- Where sellers lose time
- Why orders get cancelled or returned
- How COD confirmation currently works
- Whether an AI support agent can solve a real painful problem
